Coin Bank Project

You can register as group or as an individual for each coin bank. The "Pera Mo, Dugtong ng Buhay Ko" coin bank project proceeds will help support our kids' diagnostic tests, chemotherapy, radiotherapy and physical rehabilitation. You can register as group or as an individual for each coin bank. Your help could go a long way for the treatment of childhood cancer.
